Welcome Message

Welcome to the Minexx Marketplace, here you can purchase secutiry tokens to invest in Artisanal Mining in Central Africa. Minexx are revolutionising the Artisanal Mining industry, currently 30% of Artisanal Miners are payed with cash, for a $100Bn market, this is extremely outdated. Minexx is keen to change this, by developing the MineSmart platform, Minexx is able to track mineral purchases, on the blockchain, all the way from the buyer to the miner who mined the minerals. By establishing this network, Minexx aims to ensure all miners are fairly payed including the 40% of Miners who are currently women. Furthermore, by using the MineSmart platform, companies are able to guarantee that the minerals they have purchased have come from ethical sources in which miners are working in safe conditions and payed a fair amount. By registering miners, this prevents the explotation of children as Minexx have workers on the ground guaranteeing the ethical source of all minerals purchased via the MineSmart site. By purchasing a security token, you will be invevsting in the Artisanal Transformational Investment Fund (ATIF), the aim of the ATIF is to offer support to new mine sites to ensure they have the correct safety and mining equipment.
